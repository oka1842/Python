3D Shape Rotation Animation Interactive animation software for rotating shapes 
Keyboard shortcut keys 
Cube 1 
Triangle 2 
Tetrahedron 3 
Octahedron 4 
Cylinder 5 
Cone 6 
Sphere 7 
Torus 8 
Dodecahedron 9 
Regular Icosahedron 0
Triangular prism Q
Pentagonal pyramid W
Keyboard: 1-0,Q,W to switch shapes, Arrow keys to adjust speed, F to turn faces ON/OFF (shadow with faces ON), R to reset rotation, E to exit, F11 to full screen, ESC to cancel full screen 

Library 
tkinter 
math 
numpy
